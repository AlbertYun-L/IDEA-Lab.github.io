Dr. Yun has been awarded the College of Engineering Junior Faculty Research (JFR) Grant

======

Our project, **Transferable Deep Reinforcement Learning Across Diverse State and Action Dimensions for Energy-aware Manufacturing System Scheduling**, has been selected for the William States Lee College of Engineering Junior Faculty Research (JFR) Grants. This grant will support our research in developing transferable deep reinforcement learning frameworks that can generalize across manufacturing systems with varying state and action spaces, with a focus on energy assessment. We look forward to the exciting research ahead!
