NCROEP 2026 symposium
======
🎉 Congratulations to Omer Rehman on successfully defending his MS thesis, the first graduate from our IDEA Lab!

His work on "Battery Degradation-aware Optimal Scheduling for Hybrid Renewable Powered Micro-mobility Charging", supported by #NCROEP funding, is a great contribution to sustainable energy systems. Proud to have advised him alongside Dr. Churlzu Lim.

Excited to see Omer continue in our department as a PhD student under Dr. Fenglian Pan. The journey is just beginning! 🎓
